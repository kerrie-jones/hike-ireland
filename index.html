Benefits of Hiking

Improve Physical Health

Hiking improves fitness. Being a full body workout, it improves cardio and respiratory health, helps build stronger bones and muscles and is good for our joints. Done regularly this reduces reduces health risks such as heart disease and diabetes.

Improve Mental Health

Whether you enjoy hiking solo or as part of a group the mental health benefits to hiking are huge. Hiking makes us happier, improves sleep, and reduces stress. Alone it brings about a sense of peace and quiet and with others there is social benefits to being with a group of like minded people.

Connect with Nature

Beautiful scenery, wildlife and fresh air are good for the soul. Nature is relaxing, restorative and calming. Being in nature helps us unplug from technology and the stresses of our busy lives. "Nature does not hurry, yet everything is accomplished" Lao Tzu.

It's Free

Spending time in nature means we have to spend minimal money on our activity. Hiking is cost-effective, most hiking trails do not cost money. Hiking gear comes in a range of budgets but most people can hike as a hobby without spending and extra money
